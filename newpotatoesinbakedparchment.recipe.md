# New Potatoes in Baked Parchment

### Ingredients
 * 6 small new potatoes, scrubbed
 * 3 cloves garlic, crushed
 * 1 sprigs rosemary, chopped
 * 3 sprigs thyme, chopped
 * 4 sprigs Summer savoury, chopped
 * 1 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
 * sea salt
 * black pepper, fresh ground

### Directions

Put the potatoes in a bowl with the garlic, herbs and oil. Season and mix together well.  Place the potatoes on a sheet of baking parchment and fold into a bag (papiotte).  Bake for 40 minutes in a pre-heated oven at 200Â°C/gas 6.
