# Rice Cake


### Ingredients

 * 125 g butter
 * 250 g caster sugar
 * 3 each eggs
 * 250 g ground rice
 * 1 each lemon, grated zest

### Directions

Cream the butter and sugar. Beat in the eggs then the rice and zest. Bake
