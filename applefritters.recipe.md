# Apple Fritters

### Ingredients

 * 125 g plain flour
 * 50 g caster sugar
 * 1.5 tsp baking powder
 * 1 tsp salt
 * 1 tsp ground cinnamon
 * 80 ml milk
 * 1 each egg
 * 150g diced apple

### Directions

Whisk the dry ingredients until combined. Whisk together the egg and milk. Beat egg and milk into dry ingredients until smooth, then fold in the apple.

Heat deep fryer to 180â„ƒ. Drop a spoonful at a time of the batter into the hot oil and cook for 2-3 minutes, until golden brown.

Drain well, then toss in cinnamon sugar while still hot.
